About Ryan Bingham

Bingham is orginally from Hobbs, New Mexico, and is currently based in Los Angeles, California. His initial music career started while playing music around campfires for friends after rodeo events while he was on the rodeo circuit. He eventually began playing in small bars and honky tonks until signing with Lost Highway Records in 2007. He has since released 7 albums, American Love Song being the most recent.

Films and Awards

Bingham has appeared in movies such as Hostiles in 2017 alongside Christian Bale, and tv shows such as Yellowstone starring Kevin Costner.
Bingham's song The Weary Kind won an Oscar for Best Achievement in Music Written for Motion Pictures, Original Song for the movie Crazy Heart in 2010.
